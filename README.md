# Vrinda-Store-Data-Analysis
Objective
Vrinda Store aims to create an annual sales report for 2022 to better understand customer behavior, sales performance, and channel contribution.  
The goal is to derive actionable insights that will help increase sales and improve strategic decision-making in 2023.

Key Insights
Women customers contribute ~65% of total sales.
Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 performing states (~35% contribution).
The Adult age group (30–49 years) contributes the highest share (~50%).
Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra account for ~80% of total sales.

Business Recommendations
To increase sales:
Target women customers (30–49 years)
Focus marketing efforts in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh
Offer discounts/coupons via Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra
